MOBCELL is a leading multinational consumer electrical and mobile retailer and service company. We are the market leader in Cyprus. Founded in 2019, in a small classroom at Amdocs graduate academy, MOBCELL brings excellence in the telecommunication industry with leading and innovative products. We offer a full range of services and support, we make it easy for our customers to discover, choose and enjoy the right technology for them, throughout the life of the product. Because of our strengths, especially those that come from our scale, we are better placed than anyone to help customers to do all of that.
